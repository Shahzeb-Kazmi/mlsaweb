Fundamentals of Machine Learning

  • Type: AI
  • Date: 21 September, 2020

Summary

Learn how to get the most out of your data using Exploratory Data Analysis. Introducing you to your first unsupervised machine learning algorithm/Classifier in Python. Data is the foundation of the Digital Age. Learn how to organize, analyze and interpret these new and vast sources of information and acquire the skills to get the insight you need from your data and take better decisions. You will learn how to classify data into its own categories based on similarities without the needs for labels or labelled data. We'll teach you how to implement a K means Clustering algorithm & choose the best value for K using standard methods.
