GameDev For Beginners

  • Type: Development
  • Partner: NUST ACM
  • Date: 14 October, 2020

Summary

GameDev For Beginners was a three days workshop on game development using Unity from scratch. The workshop was taught by Danial Nasir who is a top rated freelancer and CEO at Portsea Games Day 1 was focused on setting up Unity on your PC, basic animations, assets and working with cameras Day 2 was focused on behavior of game and basic scripting using C# mainly covering the movement of the player Day 3 was focused on slightly advanced topics of Unity and proceeded towards completion
